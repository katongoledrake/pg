About Us

Our Journey to Help Vulnerable Children Heal, Grow, and Flourish in a Nurturing Environment.

'Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these.' (Mark 10:14)

A registered community based orphanage home dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for vulnerable children in need. Our mission is to empower young lives, break cycles of poverty and neglect, and foster a brighter future for generations to come. Our story began in 2021 with a vision to make a difference in the lives of children who have been orphaned, abandoned, or affected by poverty. We saw a need for a safe haven where children could find love, care, and support, and we were compelled to act.

Today, we are thriving community of children, caregivers, and supporters who share a common goal to provide a better life for those who need it most. We believe that every child deserves to grow up in a loving environment, with access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth. We are committed to creating a community that values compassion, empathy, and kindness. Our team is dedicated to providing individualized care and support, helping each child to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where every child experiences God's love, care, and protection, and grows to become a confident, compassionate, and Christ-like leader.

Mission

We rescue, care, educate, shelter and creating a self-sustaining generation of the vulnerable children by providing step down skills development, mobilizing communities towards self-independence, poverty eradication and economic sustainability.

Discipleship

We help vulnerable children discover their true identity in Christ. We believe that every child deserves to know God's love and purpose for their life.

compassion

Compassion is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that every child deserves to be loved, cared for, and treated with dignity and respect.

Health & well-being

We prioritize the health and well-being of our children, recognizing that a strong body and mind are essential for their overall development.

Community engagement

We strive to build bridges between our orphanage and the wider community, fostering relationships and partnerships that benefit everyone involved.
