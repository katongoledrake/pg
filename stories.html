Najjuko Sheila

Sheila is 10 years old girl. She has no Father and her Mother was on the verge of running away and leave her behind as she had nothing to feed her. We got the information about their situation and we choose to reach them and see how best we can help them.

Nakubulwa Elizabeth

She’s 10years old. She grew up without knowing the faces of her parents. They all died in a car accident when going to village. Elizabeth survived and got medication. She started living on the streets with a few children of similar ages when her family relatives neglected her.

Ezra

At six months, Ezra was placed outside our house around midnight by unknown person, with a swollen right Jaw wrapped in a piece of cloth and a piece of paper put in his pant which included his names and some dates which could be his date of birth. When we heard someone crying in late night, we moved outside and to find was a baby boy crying. We rescued him and welcomed him in our household. He loves everyone at home and cares for everything that has life. Please continue praying for him so that he can get a sponsor.

Shammah

She is 6 years old. When things where worse for everyone in the Lockdown, a single mother of three kids disappeared from home after failing to provide even a half a meal for her little ones, leaving them homeless and starving! When we heard news about that, we responded quickly and rescued her. It was so scary for her that even when we took her in to become part of our Family, it took several months for her to get over it, daily nightmares and up to now she hates darkness so much!

David

At just six years old, David was left at a taxi stop by his mother. He lived on that street for many days while strangers fed him, but no one was willing to take him into their home. When we learned of his situation, we brought him to the orphanage to care for him. Now you can find David laughing and enjoying stories with his new family. One day, he hopes to be a teacher, and we cannot wait to see him excelling in future!
