FAQ

What Is The Primary Focus Of Namwanja Children’s Home?

We strive to break the cycle of most vulnerable children’s lives by loving them as our own, helping them get access to all basic needs and putting an everlasting smile on the girl child, youths, women, orphans, the elderly and disabled in the community.

Can I Sponsor A Specific Child Or Project Within Namwanja Children’s Home?

Yes, you can choose to sponsor a specific child’s education or healthcare needs, or you can support a particular project within our organization. Your sponsorship allows for a direct and personal connection with the child or project you choose to support, and you will receive updates on their progress and well-being. Please contact us for more information on sponsorship opportunities.

How Can I Contribute To Namwanja Children’s Home?

You can contribute to our mission in several ways. You can make a donation through our website or participate in our fundraising events. Additionally, we welcome volunteers and partnerships with organizations that share our goals. Your support, whether financial or through your time and expertise, makes a meaningful impact on the lives of those we serve.

Why start a fundraiser with Namwanja Children’s Home?

Make a Real Difference: Your fundraiser can provide essential support to vulnerable children, improving their lives and futures. Support a Noble Cause: Namwanja Children's Home cares for orphaned, abandoned, and disadvantaged children, giving them a safe and loving environment. Meet Basic Needs: Your fundraiser can help cover basic needs like food, shelter, education, and healthcare for the children. Empower Their Future: By supporting their education and development, you can help the children build a brighter future. Community Building: Fundraising for Namwanja Children's Home can bring people together, fostering a sense of community and social responsibility. Transparency and Accountability: By partnering with a reputable organization like Namwanja Children's Home, you can ensure that funds are used efficiently and effectively.

About Us

A registered Community based orphanage home dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for vulnerable children in need. Our mission is to empower young lives, break cycles of poverty and neglect, and foster a brighter future for generations to come.
